Travel App

Designing a clean, modern travel experience that helps users explore destinations, compare places, and book trips effortlessly.

UI Design Mobile App Product Design 2024

Project Overview

The Travel App was designed to simplify how travelers explore destinations and gather important details before booking. With visually engaging place cards, smooth navigation, and detailed trip pages, the app enhances both discovery and decision-making.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Duration

4 Weeks

Tools

Figma

Team

Solo Project

Design Preview

The Challenge

Problem Statement

Users often struggle with overly complex travel apps. The challenge was to create a design that feels intuitive, lightweight, and visually appealing, while providing quick access to essential travel information like ratings, duration, climate, and pricing.

Research & Discovery

I conducted comprehensive research to understand the target audience and competitive landscape:

User Research

Through surveys and interviews with potential customers, I identified key user needs:

  • Quick search and filtering options
  • Visually rich destination cards
  • Clear pricing visibility
  • Simple navigation from browsing ‚Üí booking

Competitive Analysis

Competitor apps offered too many actions on a single page. I focused on clean spacing, strong card hierarchies, and smooth transitions to guide users through exploration naturally.

The Solution

Design Approach

I used a soft, minimal visual style with rounded components and a calm color palette to make browsing feel refreshing. Large destination cards highlight key travel details, and the booking flow is simplified into clear steps.

Key Learnings

This project reinforced several important UX principles:

  • Less is more - a clean, focused design outperforms cluttered layouts
  • User research is essential for creating effective designs
  • Micro-interactions and animations enhance user engagement when used thoughtfully
  • Mobile-first design ensures accessibility across all devices
